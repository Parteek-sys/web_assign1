Paws & Hearts Shelter

About Us

Founded in 2012, Paws & Hearts Shelter is a non-profit organization that rescues abandoned and homeless pets. Our goal is to give every animal the love, safety, and family they deserve.

With the help of volunteers and donors, we’ve found homes for over 3,000 animals. Together, we’re changing lives — one paw at a time.

Our mission is simple: to create a world where every animal has a home. Through rescue, rehabilitation, and adoption programs, we work tirelessly to match pets with loving families. We also run community awareness programs and vaccination drives to ensure the well-being of pets across our city.
